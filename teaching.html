Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy centers on creating inclusive learning environments that foster critical thinking and practical skill development. I believe in bridging the gap between theory and practice by incorporating real-world applications and industry-relevant projects into coursework.

I emphasize active learning through collaborative projects, hands-on coding experiences, and peer instruction. My goal is to develop not just technical competency, but also professional skills that prepare students for successful careers in computing.
