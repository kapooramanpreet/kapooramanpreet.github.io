Computing Education Researcher & Educator
Amanpreet Kapoor is a Ph.D. student in the Human Centered Computing program at the University of Florida. He is advised by Dr. Christina Gardner-McCune in the Engaging Learning Lab and his research interests include Computer Science Education, Informal Learning Environments, and the Future of Work.
Aman's research focuses on understanding computing students' professional identity development in formal, non-formal, and informal learning spaces.
