Computing Education Researcher & Educator
I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student in the Human Centered Computing program at the University of Florida. I am advised by Dr. Christina Gardner-McCune in the Engaging Learning Lab. My research interests include Computer Science Education, Informal Learning Environments, and Identity Formation.
